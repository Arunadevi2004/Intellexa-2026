About Symposium

INTELLEXA '26 is a premium tech fest driven by innovation, competition, and learning. Join the brightest minds across the nation to explore future technologies, build spectacular projects, and participate in high-stakes technical arenas.

About College

Ramco Institute of Technology (RIT), Rajapalayam, established in 2013 by the Raja Charity Trust, is a premier engineering institution in South India. Accredited by NAAC and NBA, RIT is committed to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation through its visionary leadership and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at RIT is a Centre of Excellence dedicated to producing socially responsible and employable engineers. With a focus on cutting-edge technologies like AI, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing, the department empowers students with deep technical expertise and professional ethics.
